Welcome To KT Chess

We host USCF-rated chess tournaments at Meadowlark Coffee & Espresso on some Saturdays from 3-7 PM, in collaboration with Meadowlark Chess Club. Registrations start at 2 PM and are limited to 12 people. Optional $1 to cover club costs is appreciated, but not required. You must be a US Chess member or sign up at the event (see their website for prices).

We also host daily USCF-rated chess tournaments at our chess.com club.
